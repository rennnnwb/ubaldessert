ICE CREAM

Persia

ice cream made with strawberry or strawberry flavoring. It is made by blending in fresh strawberries or strawberry flavoring with the eggs, cream, vanilla, and sugar used to make ice cream. Most strawberry ice cream is colored pink or light red.

Avocado Macchiato

Avocado macchiato ice cream is a delightful and unexpected flavor combination that is sure to please both coffee enthusiasts and ice cream lovers alike. Its unique blend of creamy avocado and bold macchiato makes it a refreshing and indulgent frozen treat that is perfect for enjoying on a warm day or as a delightful dessert any time of the year.

Cookies and Cream

Cookies and cream ice cream is a classic flavor that combines smooth, creamy vanilla ice cream with generous chunks of chocolate sandwich cookies. It is a delightful blend of textures and flavors that has become a favorite among ice cream enthusiasts.

Rocky Road

Rocky road ice cream is a classic and indulgent flavor that combines rich chocolate ice cream with marshmallows and nuts. It's a delightful combination of textures and flavors that has been enjoyed by ice cream lovers for decades.

Ube

Ube is a vibrant purple yam that is popular in Filipino cuisine and has gained international recognition for its unique flavor and beautiful color. Ube is not only used in savory dishes but also in various desserts, including ice cream.
